About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA), China, where I work on software engineering, DNN modularization, model reuse, etc, supervised by Prof. Hailong Sun. Before that, I earned my bachelor’s degree at Software College of Northeastern University.
